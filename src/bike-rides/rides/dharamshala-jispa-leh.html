Ladakh Bike Adventure | Highlights

About The Tour

Embark on a transcendent journey spanning 14 nights and 15 days through the enchanting landscapes of Dharamshala, Chamba, Jispa, Zanskar, and Leh. This meticulously crafted itinerary promises an odyssey of discovery, blending the marvels of nature with the thrill of adventure.

Quick Info

  • Route: Leh - Sham Valley - Khardungla - Nubra Valley - Pangong - Chang La - Leh
  • Duration: 14 Days and 13 Nights
  • Start Point: Leh Airport
  • Endpoint: Leh Airport

Inclusion

  • 14 Nights accommodation in Hotel with Double Sharing Basses
  • 14 Breakfast, 14 Dinner in Hotel
  • Backup by Tempo Traveler
  • Royal Infield Himalayan
  • 14 Day Fuel
  • Mechanic & spear part for Tour
  • Fast Aid
  • Toy train & bus tickets

Exclusion

  • Any monument entry fees / camera fees.
  • Any meal, which is not mentioned above.
  • Any air / train fare.
  • Permit Fee
  • Full Riding kit
  • Extra services to be cost
  • 5% GST

Ladakh Bike Adventure | Itinerary

Day 1 – Dharamshala Arrival
  • Morning: Arrive at Dharamshala.
  • Afternoon: Check into the hotel.
  • Evening: Test rides with motorbikes.
  • Night: Overnight in Bir Billing hotel.
Day 2 – Dharamshala to Chamba (175 km)
  • Morning: Drive to Chamba.
  • Afternoon: Explore Jot and Khajjiar.
  • Night: Overnight in Chamba hotel.
Day 3 – Chamba to Killar (166 km)
  • Morning: Drive to Killar.
  • Afternoon: Explore Tissa, Bairagarh, Sach Pass.
  • Night: Overnight in Killar hotel.
Day 4 – Killar to Jispa (155 km)
  • Morning: Drive to Jispa.
  • Afternoon: Explore Cherry, Purthi, Udaipur.
  • Night: Overnight in Jispa hotel.
Day 5 – Jispa to Padum (155 km)
  • Morning: Begin the journey to Zanskar.
  • Afternoon: Capture Shinku la pass and Gumbo Ranjan.
  • Evening: Explore Padum's attractions.
  • Night: Overnight in Padum village.
Day 6 – Padum, Zanskar Valley Local

Morning: Explore Padum's monasteries and market.

Day 7 – Padum to Lamayuru (205 km)
  • Morning: Substantial drive to Lamayuru.
  • Afternoon: Capture Shingo La pass.
  • Night: Overnight in Lamayuru.
Day 8 – Lamayuru to Leh (100 km)
  • Morning: Comfortable ride to Leh.
  • Afternoon: Visit Magnetic Hill, Pathar Sahib Gurudwara.
  • Night: Overnight in Leh.
Day 9 – Leh to Nubra Valley (160 km)
  • Morning: Scenic drive through Khardung La pass.
  • Afternoon: Explore Nubra Valley's attractions.
Day 10 – Nubra to Pangong Lake (274 km)
  • Morning: Mesmerizing journey to Pangong Lake.
  • Evening: Bonfire by the lake.
Day 11 – Pangong Lake to Hanley
  • Morning: Visit the world's highest observatory in Hanley.
Day 12 – Hanley to Umling La Pass
  • Morning: Journey to Umling La, the highest motorable road.
Day 13 – Hanley to Tso Moriri
  • Morning: Drive to Tsomoriri Lake.
Day 14 – Tso Moriri to Leh
  • Morning: Return to Leh.
Day 15 – Depart from Leh
  • Morning: Bid farewell after breakfast.
